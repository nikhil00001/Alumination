Master's Talk

The Masters' Talk is a thought-provoking session where distinguished alumni share their career trajectories, industry expertise, and personal insights. These accomplished professionals who have excelled in fields ranging from technology to business to research and policymaking, offer students a rare opportunity to learn from their experiences. Through candid discussions, they provide guidance on career growth, industry trends, and the challenges they had faced while offering valuable lessons that can shape the students' professional journeys. The session forma an excellent platform for students to seek mentorship, ask questions, and expand their professional network by connecting with industry veterans who once walked the same halls of NIT Durgapur.

Ideathon

The Ideathon is an electrifying competition that fosters creativity, problem-solving, and entrepreneurial thinking. Participants will pitch innovative ideas and disruptive solutions to real-world problems before a distinguished panel of alumni. The competition evaluates ideas based on innovation, feasibility, scalability, and impact, providing students with the opportunity to develop their strategic thinking and presentation skills. What makes this event even more exciting is the possibility of onspot funding of up to ₹50,000 for promising ideas. This creates a dynamic Shark Tank like atmosphere where students not only receive critical feedback but can also get the chance to turn their ideas into reality with the support of experienced mentors and potential investors.

CaseXpert

An intellectually stimulating event, CaseXpert, challenges students to analyze real world business models. Participants will be given complex case studies covering various domains such of business strategy, financial management, market expansion, and operational efficiency. They must work under time constraints to develop structured, well-reasoned anylitical presentation and present them before a panel of seasoned industry professionals and alumni. This competition is particularly beneficial for students aspiring to careers in consulting, management, and entrepreneurship, as it sharpens their analytical and decision-making skills. By engaging with business leaders and alumni from top firms, participants will gain firsthand exposure to the intricacies of corporate problem solving, making this an invaluable learning experience.

Linkedin BootCamp

Understanding the importance of professional branding in today's digital era, LinkedIn Bootcamp equips students with the knowledge and skills required to build an industry standard LinkedIn profile. This specialized workshop is designed to help students optimize their profiles, craft compelling headlines ,summaries while showcasing their achievements effectively. Alumni mentors provide guidance on leveraging LinkedIn for networking, job hunting, and professional growth. A well-structured LinkedIn profile is a powerful tool in the modern job market, and this bootcamp ensures that students stand out to recruiters and potential employers.

×

CV Review Camp

A strong resume is a student's gateway to internships, jobs, and higher studies, and the CV Review Camp offers a unique opportunity for students to receive one on one feedback on their resumes from alumni and industry professionals. Through personalized guidance, students learn how to structure their resumes according to current industry standards, making them more appealing to recruiters. The session covers key aspects such as ATS (Applicant Tracking System) optimization, clarity of content, and effective presentation. Alumni mentors, many of whom are hiring managers or work in top organizations will provide one on one guidance for resumes.

×

Mock-En-Joy

To further prepare students for the recruitment process, Mock-En-Joy offers a realistic interview simulation experience. Conducted by alumni from tech as well as core industries, this event replicates actual placement and internship interviews, helping students develop confidence, articulation, and problem-solving skills. Participants will be going through both technical and HR interview like questioning, while receiving immediate feedback on their performance. Industry-specific interviewers provide insights into the latest hiring trends, behavioral and situational questions, and strategies for effectively structuring responses. By experiencing the pressure and expectations of real interviews in a supportive environment, students gain the confidence needed to excel in their placement and job interviews
